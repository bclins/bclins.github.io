Course Rotation

Some courses should be offered every semester, if possible, including Math 111, 121, 140, 141, 142, and CS 261. Possibly Math 105 and CS 161 as well. Upper level courses are usually listed in the catalog as being offered in the Fall or Spring. Here is that information, as of Fall 2024

Fall Courses

  • Math 242 - Calc III
  • Math 243 - Differential Equations
  • Math 254 - Proof & Abstraction
  • Math 345 - Applied Math (even years)
  • Math 444 - Complex Analysis (even years)
  • Math 421 - Probability & Stats I (odd years)
  • Math ??? - Graph Theory (odd years)

That is 5 classes each semester if we add graph theory.

Spring Courses

  • Math 222 - Statistical Methods
  • Math 231 - Linear Algebra
  • CS 262 - Computer Science II
  • Math 323 - Engineering Mathematics (odd years)
  • Math 342 - Numerical Analysis (even years)
  • Math 422 - Probability & Stats II (even years)
  • Math 431 - Algebraic Structures (odd years)
  • Math 441 - Intermediate Analysis (even years)
  • Math/CS 460 - Algorithms (even years)
  • CS 461 - Theory of Computing (odd years)

That is 8 classes in even years, 6 in odd years. We should probably remove Math 422 and Math 323 from this list. Maybe move Math 342 to odd years? Then it would be 6 and 6.
